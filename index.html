In this exciting game you need to light as many matches as possible. Each burned match brings you points. Fire is unpredictable, as in reality, so you need to calculate every step. On the way there is a big obstacle is dynamite!!!!!!

Gameplay:

In order for the game to start, you need to open a matchbox. After opening it you will see the matches. Just swipe your finger over the matches to ignite them. Points are awarded as long as the matchstick is burning.

There are several types of matches in the game:

  • blank matches
  • standard matches
  • fireplace matches
  • tourist matches

Each match differs in the way it burns and has a different price of points when burning. Some matches have sparks that fly erratically. Take your time!!!! Be careful!!!! Dealing with fire is very difficult. Dynamite among the matches is a big obstacle in the game. When dynamite explodes the game will be over!!!!

Dynamite will explode if you:

  • touch the dynamite
  • hit the dynamite with a flame
  • it the dynamite with a spark.

Another very important difficulty is the darkening of the game field over time.

Hint: You can extinguish a match by tapping.

NOTE: matchbox game is very close to reality. The fire behaves like a real one. It is necessary to watch its spreading, the fire can escape your attention.

Be careful with fire!!!!
